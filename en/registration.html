General Ticket SmartFun Ticket

In line with Ocean Park’s reopening policy, please take note of the following new arrangements:

  1. The park’s opening hours will run from 10am to 6pm. All guests are required to make an online reservation prior to their visit. Reservations will be processed on a first-come, first-served basis. Upon arrival, guests are required to present the reservation QR code and their registered admission ticket or SmartFun Annual Pass to enter the park.
  2. Ocean Park will implement below regulations in accordance with the Government’s Prevention and Control of Disease Ordinance (Cap. 599):
    (i) Guests are required to wear face masks and undergo body temperature check upon entry.
    (ii) Face masks must be worn at all areas inside the park (except when eating).
    (iii) Park-wide capacity and capacities of individual rides and facilities will be reduced by more than half. Alternative row blocking at all theatres will be enforced.
    (iv) Tables in all restaurants will be kept at 1.5m distance.
  3. Guests who feel unwell or display any COVID-19 symptoms such as fever (>=38°C), cough, sore throat or shortness of breath are recommended to seek medical advice immediately and stay home. Ocean Park reserves the right not to admit any person displaying such symptom(s).
  4. Guests should not visit the park if they have a family member or live with someone who displays COVID-19 symptom(s).

Online Reservation Procedure

  1. All guests and SmartFun Annual Pass members must make an online reservation prior to visiting the park, except those with exemption.
  2. Guests exempted from reservation include:
    (i) Hong Kong residents aged 65 or above
    (ii) Hong Kong residents aged 65 or above with at most two accompanying children aged 3-11
  3. Each reservation can include a maximum of 8 persons. Due to the capacity limitation, reservation will be processed on a first-come, first-served basis.
  4. The last 4 characters of the admission ticket or SmartFun Annual Pass barcode number must be filled in at the time of registration. In addition, a valid email address must be provided for receiving the reservation notification.
  5. Upon successful reservation, guests will receive a reservation QR code on the same screen, followed by an email notification. Upon entry, guests must present both the reservation QR code and registered admission ticket or SmartFun Annual Pass. The reservation QR code is only valid for one-time entry on the registered date. All registrants must enter the park at the same time.
  6. Once the reservation is complete, the registered information cannot be altered.
  7. Ocean Park reserves the right to amend the park’s admission procedures subject to weather conditions or actual circumstances without prior notice. If guests are affected and not able to enter the park, their reservation QR code will also be considered invalid. Guests will need to make another reservation.
  8. Data collected for reservation purpose will only be used for disease prevention and control measure implementation. The park reserves the right to use the collected data for related incidents where necessary. Please click here for the Personal Information Collection Statement.
  9. If you have any enquiry, please contact the park’s Guest Relations team at (852) 3923 2323 from 9am to 6pm.
