In line with Ocean Park’s reopening policy, please take note of the following new arrangements:

  1. The Park’s opening hour is from 10am to 6pm. All guests are required to make advance online reservation for entering the Park. Reservations will be processed on a first-come, first-served basis.
  2. Guests are required to wear face masks and undergo body temperature checks upon entry.
  3. Face masks must be worn at all areas inside the Park (except when dining). Face masks are available for purchase at designated locations. All proceeds after deducting costs will go to Ocean Park Conservation Foundation, Hong Kong.
  4. Park-wide capacity and capacity of individual rides and facilities will be reduced by more than half. Alternative row blocking at all theatres will be enforced.
  5. Tables in all restaurants will be kept at a distance of 1.5m apart with no more than 8 persons seated together at one table. This arrangement will be adjusted in accordance with updates of the Government’s Prevention and Control of Disease (Prohibition on Group Gathering) Regulation.
  6. Guests must comply with the Government’s Prevention and Control of Disease (Prohibition on Group Gathering) Regulation (Cap 599G) on the maximum number of persons for group gatherings as stipulated by the law.
  7. Guests who feel unwell or display any COVID-19 symptoms such as fever (>=38°C), cough, sore throat or shortness of breath are recommended to seek medical advice immediately and stay home. Ocean Park reserves the right not to admit any people displaying such symptoms.
  8. Guests should not visit the park if they have a family member or friend living together has the COVID-19 symptoms.

Advance Reservation Procedure

  1. All guests and SmartFun annual pass members must make advance reservation prior to visiting the Park, except those with exemption.
  2. Guests who will be exempted from reservation:
    (i) Hong Kong residents aged 65 or above
    (ii) Hong Kong residents aged 65 or above with no more than two accompanying children aged 3-11
  3. Each registration can include a maximum of 8 persons. Advance reservation can be made up to 14 days. Due to daily quota limitation, reservation will be on a first-come, first-served basis.
  4. The last 4 digits of the admission ticket or SmartFun annual pass barcode must be filled in at the time of registration, plus a valid email address (same email address can only be used for reservation once per day).
  5. Guests will receive an instant QR code and email notification upon successful reservation. Upon entry, guests must present both the QR code and admission ticket or SmartFun annual pass used for registration. The QR code is only valid for one-time entry. All registrants must enter the park at the same time.
  6. Once reservation is completed, the registered information cannot be altered.
  7. If you have any enquiry, please contact Guest Relations at (852) 3923 2323 during 9am to 6pm.
  8. Ocean Park reserves the rights to change the admission procedures according to actual circumstances without prior notice.
