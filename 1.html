Online Registration Procedure

  1. All guests and SmartFun annual pass members must register online prior to visiting, except those with exemption.
  2. Below persons are exempted from registration:
    (i) Hong Kong resident aged 65 or above
    (ii) Hong Kong resident aged 65 or above with two accompanying children aged between 3-11
  3. Each registration can include a maximum of 8 persons. Advance registration can be made up to 14 days. Due to daily quota limitation, registration is on first-come, first-served basis.
  4. Registration requires the provision of admission ticket or SmartFun annual pass number, as well as an email address (same email address can only be registered once per day).
  5. Guests will receive email notification upon successful registration. Guests must present both the notification email and admission ticket or SmartFun annual pass during entry, and enter the Park with other registrants at the same time.
  6. Registration cannot be altered. If required, please fill out a new registration.
